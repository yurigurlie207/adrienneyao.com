ASIA TUINA NY

• • • • • • • • • •

Asia Tuina is a massage and acupuncture shop in New York City. Their mission is to provide skilled deep-tissue massage therapy, and holistic care in a relaxing atmosphere.

While we wanted a full website perfect for the desktop browser, much of Asia Tuina's clientele find out about the shop through their mobile devices. It became especially crucial that the website's content is easily accessible through various mobile devices.

RESPONSIVE LAYOUT

• • • • • • • • • •

The biggest challenge of this project was figuring out how to present all the information in an effective manner given the different browser sizes. We wanted to ensure that the most important information was easily visible, and if not, can be easily navigated.

For Asia Tuina, we focused on making sure the main services were apparent, and the pricing information was easily accessible.

MISC.

• • • • • • • • • •

  • Creating light graphics used throughout the site.
